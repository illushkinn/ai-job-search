## CV Structure Rules

- **Format:** Modern, clean layout. Markdown for source, LaTeX for PDF output.
- **Length:** 2 pages maximum for experienced roles, 1 page for targeted applications.
- **Section order:** About / Profile → Experience (most recent first) → Education → Key Skills → Languages → Volunteer / Extras
- **Tone:** Active first-person for Spanish CVs, direct verbs (no "I") for English CVs
- **Metrics:** Include concrete numbers where possible (6 LPs in 3 months, 10-20 evaluations/week, $500k ARS/month)
- **Honesty rule:** Never fabricate or exaggerate. Gaps are addressed honestly, not hidden.

## Role-Specific Tailoring Notes

### For Consultancy Roles (Globant, Accenture, Deloitte)
- Emphasize: client-facing experience, project coordination, AI integration, WCAG accessibility
- De-emphasize: solo freelance work, side projects (unless directly relevant)
- Highlight: bilingual/multilingual capability for cross-border projects

### For Agency Roles (Creative, Russell Bedford, boutiques)
- Emphasize: The Brand Crew (direct agency experience), 6 landing pages in 3 months, design systems
- De-emphasize: Stock para tu Negocio (unless relevant to the client)
- Highlight: speed of delivery, AI-powered workflow, client communication

### For Product Roles (MELI, Ualá, Despegar)
- Emphasize: product planning, sprint coordination, stakeholder communication, consumer psychology
- De-emphasize: frontend development details (focus on product decisions)
- Highlight: data-informed decisions, cross-functional team coordination

### For AI-Focused Roles
- Emphasize: Outlier (1 year of LLM evaluation), Pragma (voice assistant), bias detection
- De-emphasize: pure design work
- Highlight: understanding of AI limitations, evaluation methodology, agent architecture thinking

### For NZ / International Roles
- Emphasize: multilingual capability, cross-cultural work (UNDP translation volunteer), remote collaboration
- De-emphasize: local-only experience
- Highlight: adaptability, cultural bridge capability, audit-proof skills
- Add note about upcoming NZ work & Travel visa (availability to start)

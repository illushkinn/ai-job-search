## Workflow for New Job Applications
1. User provides a job posting (URL or text)
2. **Always evaluate fit first**: skills match, experience match, behavioral/culture match, location alignment, career goals
3. If good fit: create tailored CV + cover letter
4. Verify both documents against profile
5. Prepare interview talking points based on role requirements and strengths

## Verification Checklist
After creating or updating a CV or cover letter:
- [ ] All claims match actual profile — no fabricated skills or experience
- [ ] Job titles, dates, company names, and locations are correct
- [ ] Contact details are correct
- [ ] Profile statement is tailored to the specific role
- [ ] Skills and experience reframed to match job requirements
- [ ] Key requirements addressed (gaps acknowledged, not hidden)
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors
- [ ] Consistent tone across CV and cover letter
- [ ] CV fits appropriate length, cover letter fits one page
